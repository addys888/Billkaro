PRO TIPS
Tips & Best Practices
Get the most out of BillKaro with these expert tips.
💡
Send invoices immediately
Create and send the invoice right when the work is done. Don't delay — faster invoicing means faster payment!
💡
Use voice for speed
When you're on-site or busy, just send a voice note. It's 3x faster than typing and the AI handles everything.
💡
Check your dashboard weekly
Visit the dashboard to see your financial overview — total revenue, pending amounts, overdue invoices, and client history.
💡
Include item names
Be specific: "Bill 5000 to Rahul for AC repair, gas refill" is better than just "Bill 5000 to Rahul". It helps both you and your client.
💡
Complete your business profile
Add GSTIN, address, and bank details for the most professional-looking invoices. Clients trust businesses with complete details.
